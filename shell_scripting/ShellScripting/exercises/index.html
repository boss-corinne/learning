Shell Scripting - Exercises

There are typically more exercises to attempt than the time allocated during the course allows. Do as much as you can do, or want to do, in the time available, and try and find time after the course has finished to return to complete these exercises.

Ask your instructor for help or advice at any time.

At the end of the exercise session your instructor will run through any essential exercises before resuming the formal teaching. Please ask you instructor to review any other exercises you would like further information about.

Your instructor will let you know which exercises you are expected to undertake, but as a general guide the links below correspond to each chapter of the training course that has supporting exercises.
